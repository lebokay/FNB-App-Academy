hulk

Dr. Robert Bruce Banner

His core ability is unlimited strength that increases the angrier he gets, making him one of the strongest beings in the Marvel Universe. He is nearly invulnerable, with skin that resists bullets, explosions, and extreme conditions. The Hulk also possesses an accelerated healing factor, can leap vast distances, and adapt to survive in harsh environments like deep space or underwater. In his most powerful forms, he can emit gamma radiation and cause massive destruction with thunderclaps and shockwaves.

captain america

steve rogers

super-soldier and iconic leader of the Avengers in the Marvel Universe. Originally a frail young man during World War II, he was transformed into the peak of human potential through the Super Soldier Serum. This gave him enhanced strength, speed, agility, endurance, reflexes, and a healing factor—though not to the level of superhuman beings like Hulk or Thor. He is a master martial artist, expert tactician, and wields an indestructible vibranium shield that he uses for both defense and offense with incredible precision. Known for his unwavering moral compass, leadership, and bravery, Captain America embodies the ideal of a hero—fighting not just with strength, but with heart and conviction.r

black widow

Natasha Romanoff

a highly skilled spy, assassin, and combat expert in the Marvel Universe. Originally trained in the secretive Russian "Red Room" program, she was conditioned to be a lethal operative from a young age. While she doesn't possess superhuman powers, she operates at peak human levels of strength, agility, reflexes, and endurance. Natasha is a master of martial arts, weapons, and stealth, and is fluent in multiple languages. She’s also an expert hacker and strategist, making her a key asset in intelligence missions. Her combat gear often includes electric "Widow's Bite" wrist gauntlets. Despite her dark past, Black Widow fights for redemption and justice, and her courage and intelligence make her a vital member of the Avengers.
